My name is Jucelle. A designer, illustrator, observer and master doodler currently residing in San Francisco Bay Area. I am easy to get along with and enjoy collaborating with fellow problem solvers and adventurers.

Being creative is what I enjoy the most as it allows me to communicate my thoughts and share them with everyone. Each project that I produce is approached with intelligent, thoughtful and well-informed ideations. Majority of my work is centered around branding, identities, graphics, packaging and print.

Currently infatuated by watercolors, hand drawn letterforms, textures, organic-style vectors and Starbucks refreshers.
